Frequently asked questions

For any unanswered questions, reach out to our support team via email. We'll respond as soon as possible to assist you.

How do I sign up?

Download the app from the Play Store or App Store, create an account, and enter your referral code (if you have one).

What can I do with this app?

You can log blood pressure and blood sugar readings, get insights and reminders, and share progress with your doctor.

How does this app help my doctor?

It allows your doctor to view your readings remotely and make informed decisions between visits.

Can I use the app without a doctor?

Yes, you can use it for personal tracking. Having a doctor linked just enhances the support you receive.

Can I use the app without a doctor?

Only you and the health professional you choose to share it with. We never sell or share your data with third parties.
